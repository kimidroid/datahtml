Lucky Blocks Race (Map/Minigame)

Lucky Block Race is a classic mini racing game with lucky blocks.

The game consists of each player having to advance on their respective track, while destroying the lucky blocks that will be on the way. Remember that destroying a lucky block will give you random things that can be good or bad. Also at the end you will be able to fight with your friends, since this map includes a small pvp arena.

- You can play this map alone or with friends
- Supports up to 8 players in 4 duos
- Includes a reset button to play it as many times as you want
- Pvp arena is only available when there are more than one player.
